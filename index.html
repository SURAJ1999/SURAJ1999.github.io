    SOME FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

    Who all can participiate ?

    Any student from any technical institution

    Any student from any technical institution, irrespective of their branch and year of study can participiate in SANKET'19. However, in group events all the team members of a group should be from the same institution.

    What is maximum number of team members ?

    Well, the maximum is 4

    Although the maximum is four per team, there is no restriction on the minimum number of team members, even an individual can participate in most events. However the registration fees will be the same irrespective of number of team members.

    Will certificates be provided ?

    Yeah, certificates will be provided

    Certificates will be provided after completing the workshops and upon reaching the finals of the event. It is mandatory to complete the workshop to get certificates in the same.

    How is registration done ?

    Registrations are done online

    The registrations and payments for the same are done online, and due to the limited number of seats in workshops, the registrations are done on a first come first serve basis.
